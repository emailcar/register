Town  Introduction
Guzhen--the Lighting Capital of China, is the biggest professional production base and terminal market of lighting in China. More than 8,000 lighting enterprises are registered in Guzhen,accounting for over 60% of the market share in China, forming a world-famous industrial cluster. Guzhen lighting products are sold to more than 130 countries and regions around the world.
As an annual event, the 11th China (Cuzhen) International lighting Fair & LED Application Show has attracted 614 exhibitors and 64,138 professional buyers form 103 countries and regions around the world. The 12th Lighting Fair will be held at the new Guzhen Exhibition Center, which covers over 30,000 square meters with more than 1,500 standard booths.
The 12th China(Guzhen) Internation Lighting Fair & LED Application Show will feature the environmental concept, and incite more than 20 professional media and websites and over 30 domestic mass media to cover the event.
The 12th China(Guzhen) International Ligthing Fair & LED Application Show will be a platform for etablishing company image, presenting product information, and searching for ideal partners. We look forward to your visit!
